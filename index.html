Nike

Nike is a global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Our mission is to bring innovation and inspiration to every athlete in the world. We believe in the power of sport to unite people, inspire positive change, and break down barriers. With a commitment to diversity, inclusion, and equality, we revolutionize the sports industry with iconic products that redefine athletic performance and style. Join us on our journey to unleash the potential of every athlete and make a difference through the power of sport.

The iconic Nike swoosh logo was designed by Carolyn Davidson in 1971 and was purchased by Nike for just $35

The Air Jordan line of sneakers, created in collaboration with basketball legend Michael Jordan, revolutionized the sneaker industry and became one of Nike's most successful product lines

The company has a significant presence in the global sportswear market and operates retail stores and online platforms in over 170 countries.

Nike, Inc. is a renowned multinational corporation specializing in the design, development, manufacturing, and marketing of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Founded on January 25, 1964, as Blue Ribbon Sports (BRS) by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has since become a global leader in the sports industry.

Nike's journey is characterized by its relentless pursuit of innovation, commitment to performance, and dedication to serving athletes and enthusiasts worldwide. With a rich history spanning several decades, Nike continues to shape the future of sportswear and inspire individuals to unleash their full potential, both on and off the field.
